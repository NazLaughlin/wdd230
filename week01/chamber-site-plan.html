Site Plan

Site Name

Seymour Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Seymour Chamber of Commerce works to promote businesses owned by locals and events to further the unity of the community.

Scenarios

  • What events will be hosted this month?
  • How can my business join the Chamber?
  • What is the main economic driver of the city?

Color Schema

The main background will be Peach Yellow: #F7DBA7, with most of the text being Van Dyke: #523F38. Menus and other highlights will have a Honolulu Blue background: #0E79B2 or Pine Green: #037971, and for better contrast text in these places will be Periwinkle: #C2BBF0.

Typography

The font family Libre Baskerville will be used for headers and menus, while the family Cabin will be used in all other places.
